Our Story Starts Here

Based in the Lehigh Valley, The Loaded fry started out as nothing more than a food vendor at local events like Musikfest, Allentown fair, Blues and Brews and more. Now with a location in southside Bethlehem we are able to serve the people the junk food of their dreams year round! What was once a father and son duo is now a dedicated working group of employees that enjoy junk food just like the rest of the world. Coming in you are greeted with a judgement free zone and ultimate selections to choose from. All our fries are hand cut and never frozen for the perfect savory taste that comes with the perfect stack of fries. All dipping sauces are made in-house to ensure each dunk of fry is covered in goodness. And of course, all the cheese we use is shredded daily to perfection! We also specialize in golden chicken tenders, mac and cheese, mozzarella sticks, 100% beef burgers and even soft serve icecream. So looking for spot to indulge in the best comfort food in the area? Then come down to The Loaded Fry and succumb to your stress relieveing food!
